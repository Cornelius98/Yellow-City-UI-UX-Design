About Kabwata Ward

Residential wards provide residents with a more localized level of representation and engagement in local government. This can help residents voice their concerns and influence decisions that affect their specific neighborhoods.
Residential wards may play a role in local land-use planning and zoning regulations. Decisions related to land development, zoning changes, and building permits often involve input and considerations at the ward level.

KABWATA COMMUNITY STATEMENTS

The Kabwata Community is committed to ensure the; mission statement, the vision statement, the motto statement and the peace Proclamation of Kabwata Ward is upheld always.

Mission Statement
Our mission is to provide exemplary public service and maintain the highest standards of integrity, transparency, and accountability. We are dedicated to fostering a safe, inclusive, and vibrant community, and to managing resources efficiently for the well-being and prosperity of our residents.
Vision Statement
Our vision is to protect human health and the environment through effective regulation and stewardship. We work to reduce pollution, enforce environmental laws, and promote sustainable practices to ensure a cleaner, healthier, and more resilient world for current and future generations
Motto Statement
Justice, Equity, and Integrity.
Peace Proclamation
Whereas, we acknowledge the imperative need for dialogue, reconciliation, and cooperation among nations, communities, and individuals to build a more peaceful and just world. Therefore, let it be known that we, the signatories of this peace proclamation, commit ourselves.
